Recommended Remedies

Healthy Crop Tips

💧

Water Wisely

Water at the base of plants early morning to reduce leaf moisture and prevent fungal spread.

✂️

Prune Regularly

Remove infected leaves immediately and dispose away from the garden to stop contamination.

🔄

Rotate Crops

Changing planting locations each season reduces soil-borne diseases and replenishes nutrients.

🌬️

Ensure Air Flow

Proper plant spacing allows air circulation, reducing humidity that promotes disease growth.
